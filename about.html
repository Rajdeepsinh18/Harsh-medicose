About Us

Harsh Medicose Pvt. Ltd.

Harsh Medicose Pvt. Ltd is a Gujarat based pharmaceutical company with scientifically strong and research based innovative ideas derived by vigorous efforts. We are a team of dynamic people who believe that healthcare is the most fundamental and imperative right of a human. Healthcare needs to be strategically planned in a way that the basic right of human is met with due care and diligence.

At Harsh Medicose, we are driven by a single motto, the motto to value human life. With the same idea put ahead of all other goals, we have planned our strategic ventures to establish capabilities to innovate new products that are ahead of the market standards still being competent and affordable.

Harsh Medicose Pvt Ltd is a strong contender in the pharmaceutical sector with a dedication to develop and derive therapeutically proven range of generic pharmaceutical

products and supply them to the patients at affordable prices. Our extensive product line promises to cater to the needs of various healthcare segments including generic medicines, surgical products and medically proven herbal cosmetics. With an open minded approach towards creative innovation using advanced medical technology, we operate beyond and across the Indian shores to cater the healthcare needs of various other nations through our exports. We have an established network of logistics that enables quality sourcing of medical products from various countries.
