Harsh Medicose Pvt. Ltd. and Harsh International LLC is a customer centric pharmaceutical company providing an array of quality healthcare services across the globe. We are the visionary with a primary goal to serve humanity with wide range of pharmaceutical products that are competent in the market and serve the need for better healthcare production facilities. We are well versed in understanding the market requirement and our experts are keen to work in challenging conditions. Our logistics are well in place to serve the market in critical conditions. Operational excellence and cost competitive manufacturing is key to better products and services.

We hold expertise in the erection of Green Field Projects for the formulation of Pharmaceutical Products and also manufacturing drugs in bulk. Our dedicated team of expert pharmacist and engineers work together in bringing solutions that are medically sound and technically ahead of the market. We have a thorough understanding about pharmaceutical plants and we provide guidance for upgrading plants at minimal price. We are well versed in providing quality solution and managing systems. Our team meticulously work on documentation, validation, training, preparing regulatory audits and other issues related to regulations regarding quality system. We work in compliance with the norms laid by the government and our team has in-depth understanding for REVISED SCHEDUL–EM, WHO-GMP, TGA (Australia), MCC (South Africa), MHRA (UK), EU / EDQM, USFDA & Other African countries
